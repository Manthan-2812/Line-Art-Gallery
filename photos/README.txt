PHOTOS FOLDER
=============
Paste your art images here with these exact filenames:

  art1.jpg  — shown on DraggableCards in the hero section (card 1)
  art2.jpg  — DraggableCards card 2
  art3.jpg  — DraggableCards card 3
  art4.jpg  — DraggableCards card 4
  art5.jpg  — DraggableCards card 5

  profile.jpg — YOUR PHOTO used by PixelatedCanvas in the About Me section
                (recommended size: 400×500 px, portrait orientation)

JPG, PNG, and WEBP are all fine.
If a file is missing the card shows a dark fallback tile.
